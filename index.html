Before You Visit

Share Your Requirement for a Better Salon Consultation

Providing a few quick details ahead of time allows our salon team to prepare suitable products, advise on timing, and give you exact pricing quotes during your consultation.

✓
Required service: Specific cut, colour technique, spa or grooming treatment.
✓
Preferred date: Day you plan to visit the salon.
✓
Preferred time: Morning, afternoon, or evening timing preference.
✓
Hair length or service area: Short, medium, long hair, or target body area.
✓
Reference photograph: Any inspirational haircut or colour photos you wish to share.
✓
Chemical-service history or sensitivities: Prior keratin, smoothing, or skin sensitivities.

Safety Notice: For colour, straightening, smoothing, keratin, waxing or skin-related services, inform the salon about allergies, sensitivities, recent procedures, injuries or other special-care requirements.
